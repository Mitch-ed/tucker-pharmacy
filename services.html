Our Services

Full-service pharmacy care designed around you — from the routine to the complex.

What We Offer

Everything You Need,
Under One Roof

Tucker Pharmacy offers a full range of pharmacy services — all delivered with the personal attention that only a locally owned, independent pharmacy can provide. If you have a need we haven't listed here, just ask. We are always glad to help.

Prescription Refills

Getting your prescription refilled should be simple and stress-free. You can request a refill by phone, through our mobile app, or simply by stopping in. We'll have it ready when you need it. For maintenance medications, we can help set up automatic refill reminders so you never run out unexpectedly.

New Patient Services

Switching to Tucker Pharmacy is easy. Our team will handle the coordination with your doctor's office and your previous pharmacy so the transition is smooth and uninterrupted. We'll review your complete medication list, answer your questions, and make sure you feel comfortable with your new pharmacy home from day one.

Medication Counseling

Starting a new medication? Have questions about side effects, dosing, or how your prescriptions interact with each other? Our pharmacists are always available to talk. We believe an informed patient is a healthier patient, and we take time to make sure you fully understand every medication you're taking — what it does, how to take it, and what to watch for.

Speak with a Pharmacist

Immunizations & Vaccinations

Staying up to date on vaccinations is one of the most important things you can do for your health and the health of those around you. Tucker Pharmacy administers a range of vaccines including flu shots, pneumonia vaccines, shingles vaccines, and more. No appointment necessary for most vaccinations — just stop in during regular hours. Please call ahead to confirm availability and current vaccine offerings.

Check Vaccine Availability

Prescription Transfers

Transferring your prescriptions from another pharmacy is quick, easy, and free. Simply bring in your current prescription bottles or contact information for your previous pharmacy, and we'll take care of the rest. In most cases, your prescriptions can be transferred and ready for pickup the same day. We accept transfers from all major pharmacy chains and independent pharmacies.

Start a Transfer

Over-the-Counter Guidance

The pharmacy aisle can be overwhelming. Our team is here to help you cut through the options and find what actually works for your situation. Whether you're managing a cold, looking for a pain reliever that won't interact with your medications, or need guidance on vitamins and supplements, we're happy to offer personalized, practical recommendations. No question is too small.
