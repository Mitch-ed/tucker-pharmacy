PocketRx App

Mobile App

Manage Your Prescriptions from Anywhere

The PocketRx mobile app is designed to make managing your medications as easy as possible. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, you can request refills, check prescription status, and stay organized — all without making a phone call or waiting in line.

The app is available for iPhone and Android and connects directly to your Tucker Pharmacy account. Setup takes just a few minutes, and once you're in, everything is in one convenient place.

Features

Everything You Need, Built In

The PocketRx app is straightforward and designed with patients in mind — not complexity.

One-Tap Refills

Request a refill on any of your active prescriptions with a single tap. We'll notify you when it's ready for pickup.

Prescription History

View all of your current and past prescriptions in one organized list. Great for keeping track of what you take and when.

Refill Reminders

Set automatic reminders so you never run out of a medication. The app can alert you a few days before you're due to run low.

Pickup Notifications

Get notified as soon as your prescription is filled and ready. No more guessing — you'll know the moment it's good to go.

Family Account Management

Manage prescriptions for your entire family from a single app account. Ideal for parents managing medications for children.

Hours & Contact

View Tucker Pharmacy's hours, contact info, and location directly in the app. Call or email us with one tap.

Getting Started

Up and Running in Minutes

Getting set up with the PocketRx app is quick and easy. You don't need any technical knowledge — just a smartphone and a few minutes.

1

Download the App

Search for "PocketRx" in the App Store or Google Play, or use the download links on this page to find it directly.

2

Create Your Account

Register with your name, date of birth, and the phone number on file at Tucker Pharmacy. Your prescriptions will sync automatically.

3

Manage Your Medications

Request refills, set reminders, and view your prescription history — all from the app. It's that simple.

4

Pick Up In Store

We'll send a notification when your prescription is ready. Swing by during store hours and you're all set.

Questions About the App?

We're Happy to Help You Get Set Up

If you have trouble downloading or logging in, just give us a call or stop by. Our team is glad to walk you through it in person.
